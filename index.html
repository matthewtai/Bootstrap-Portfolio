About Me

Hi, I'm Matthew Tai and I am a professional chef.

I've started working in the industry since 2013 and I currently work at the Sheraton in downtown Toronto. I am very honoured to be a part of Bocuse d'Or Canada since late 2015. In order to better assist my team, I started designing stencils/moulds and recently expanded my services. My current clients include the Delta Hotels Toronto, Sheraton Centre Toronto as well as Global Chef Canada. Recently, our team travelled to Mexico City in April 2018 to compete in the Bocuse d'Or Americas 2018 qualifiers and we came second. We will be going to Lyon, France in 2019 to compete in the finals against 23 other countries.
